## Strategy (high level)
- **Primary win condition:** Snowball pressure with **Rain-boosted Pelipper** enabling **Archaludon Electro Shot** turns, while **Incineroar + redirection** keep tempo.
- **Speed control:** Tailwind (Pelipper) + natural priority (Thunderclap) + board control (Spore/Parting Shot).
- **Defensive glue:** Intimidate, Parting Shot, double redirection (Amoonguss + Ogerpon Follow Me) depending on the matchup.

## Modes & common lines
### Mode A: Standard Rain + Tailwind
- **Bring:** Pelipper + Archaludon + Incineroar + (Raging Bolt / Ogerpon-W / Amoonguss)
- **Common leads:** Pelipper+Archaludon, Pelipper+Incineroar
- **Typical turn 1:** Tailwind or Wide Guard with Pelipper while Archaludon starts threatening Electro Shot.

### Mode B: Bulky positioning / slow grind
- **Bring:** Incineroar + Amoonguss + Raging Bolt + Archaludon
- **Common leads:** Incineroar+Amoonguss, Incineroar+Raging Bolt
- **Typical turn 1:** Fake Out / Rage Powder to scout, then pivot Parting Shot and look for Spore windows.

## Terastallization plan
- **Primary Tera users:** Archaludon (Grass), Raging Bolt (Fairy), Incineroar (Grass)
- **Notes:**
	- **Archaludon — Tera Grass:** patches Ground weaknesses and helps stay on the field to stack Stamina value.
	- **Raging Bolt — Tera Fairy:** improves into Dragons/Fighting pressure while keeping Thunderclap endgames.
	- **Pelipper — Tera Ghost:** emergency vs Fake Out/Normal/Fighting interactions to preserve Tailwind.

## IV notes
- Special attackers carry **IVs: 0 Atk** to reduce damage from Foul Play/confusion.
- Amoonguss keeps **0 Spe** to function better in Trick Room pivots.
