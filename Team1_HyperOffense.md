# Team 1: Hyper Offense with Tailwind

## Strategy
This team focuses on speed control with Tailwind and high offensive pressure. Flutter Mane and Urshifu-R provide early aggression, supported by the utility of Tornadus-T. Rillaboom and Chi-Yu add balance, while Landorus-T provides additional coverage and Intimidate support.
