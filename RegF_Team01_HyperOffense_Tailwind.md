## Strategy (high level)
- **Primary win condition:** Create immediate pressure with Specs Flutter Mane + Band Urshifu-R while Tornadus provides Tailwind and disruption (Taunt).
- **Speed control:** Tailwind.
- **Damage plan:** Double-target key pieces early; use spread pressure from Chi-Yu Heat Wave and Landorus Rock Slide when it’s safe.
- **Endgames:** Priority cleanup with Grassy Glide/Aqua Jet; scarf Landorus closes games once opposing speed control is removed.
- **Defensive tools:** Intimidate (Landorus), Fake Out (Rillaboom), Taunt (Tornadus), Water Teras to patch Fire/Ice matchups.

## Modes & common lines
### Mode A: Standard Tailwind offense
- **Bring:** Tornadus + Flutter Mane + Urshifu-R + (Rillaboom / Chi-Yu)
- **Common leads:** Tornadus+Flutter, Tornadus+Urshifu
- **Typical turn 1:** Tailwind or Taunt with Tornadus; pressure KOs with Specs/Band.

### Mode B: Anti-balance / spread pressure
- **Bring:** Tornadus + Chi-Yu + Landorus-T + Rillaboom
- **Common leads:** Tornadus+Chi-Yu, Tornadus+Landorus
- **Typical turn 1:** Tailwind; start Heat Wave / Rock Slide cycles while pivoting with U-turn.

## Terastallization plan
- **Primary Tera users:** Urshifu-R (Water), Flutter Mane (Ghost), Rillaboom (Water)
- **Common Teras:**
	- **Urshifu-R — Tera Water:** maximizes Surging Strikes and improves defensive profile.
	- **Rillaboom — Tera Water:** improves vs Fire/Ice and keeps Fake Out/Glide utility.
	- **Flutter Mane — Tera Ghost:** stronger Shadow Ball lines into bulky Psychics.
- **Emergency Teras:** Tornadus (Water) to survive priority/Ice and keep Tailwind.

## Benchmarks / EV notes
- Simple max/max spreads to maximize speed + damage under Tailwind.

## Matchups
### Good into
- Slower balance teams without strong Tailwind denial.

### Even / skill
- Other Tailwind mirrors.

### Struggles vs
- Hard Trick Room if Taunt is denied.
- **Fixes / options:** consider Encore/Thunder Wave on Tornadus or a dedicated TR denial slot if you rebuild the roster.
