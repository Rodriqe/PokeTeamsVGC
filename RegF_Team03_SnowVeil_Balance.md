## Strategy (high level)
- **Primary win condition:** Establish **Aurora Veil** early, then convert the extra bulk into a winning board state via **Gholdengo Nasty Plot** or **Baxcalibur** pressure.
- **Speed control:** Icy Wind + natural speed tiers; priority cleanup with Ice Shard.
- **Defensive tools:** Aurora Veil, Intimidate + Parting Shot, dual Follow Me/Fake Out lines.

## Modes & common lines
### Mode A: Veil -> Setup
- **Bring:** Ninetales-A + Gholdengo + Incineroar + (Ogerpon-W / Iron Hands)
- **Common leads:** Ninetales-A+Incineroar, Ninetales-A+Ogerpon-W
- **Typical turn 1:** Aurora Veil if safe; otherwise Icy Wind + pivot (Parting Shot) to re-enable a cleaner Veil.

### Mode B: Baxcalibur pressure
- **Bring:** Ninetales-A + Baxcalibur + Incineroar + Ogerpon-W
- **Common leads:** Ninetales-A+Baxcalibur, Incineroar+Ogerpon-W
- **Typical turn 1:** Veil or Icy Wind; trade aggressively while Veil is up, then endgame with Ice Shard.

## Terastallization plan
- **Primary Tera users:** Gholdengo (Steel), Baxcalibur (Dragon), Incineroar/Iron Hands (Grass)
- **Notes:**
	- **Gholdengo — Tera Steel:** maximizes Make It Rain damage to close games under Veil.
	- **Ninetales-A — Tera Ghost:** emergency to preserve Veil turns into Fake Out / Fighting pressure.
	- **Baxcalibur — Tera Dragon:** stronger Glaive Rush lines and cleaner late-game sweeps.

## IV notes
- Special attackers carry **IVs: 0 Atk** to reduce Foul Play/confusion damage.
- Iron Hands keeps **0 Spe** to be less awkward into Trick Room pivots.
